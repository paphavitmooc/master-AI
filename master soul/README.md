## How NEXUS Works

1. **Alex brings a task or challenge** (any domain)
2. **NEXUS analyses** — understands requirement, identifies risks, selects agent(s)
3. **NEXUS delegates** — structured brief with clear deliverables and deadline
4. **NEXUS tracks** — checkpoint at 50% of deadline or 24h
5. **NEXUS reviews** — quality check before output reaches Alex
6. **NEXUS reports** — clean summary, next actions, status
